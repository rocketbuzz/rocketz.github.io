Overview and Excerpt

A biological researcher has found that certain bacterial species found in the belly buttons of humans can potentially be used to create food that tastes like beef. There are various types of bacterial species that live on the human body and these species also vary from person to person. The biologist has collected information from people all over the United States on the type of bacterial species that live in their navel.
